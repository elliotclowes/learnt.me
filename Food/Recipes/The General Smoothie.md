This is a health smoothie I make.

185g fruits, 100g yogurt, 13g nuts, and 25g protein powder.

## Ingredients
- Raspberries (40g)
- Blueberries (40g)
- Banana (85g)
- Whey Protein (35g)
- Spinach (10g)
- Greek Yogurt (100g)
- Almond Milk (200ml)
- Flaxseed (3g)
- Brazil Nuts (5g)
- Walnuts (5g)
- Macadamia Nuts (5g)
